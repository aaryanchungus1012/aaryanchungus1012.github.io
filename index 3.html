About Classmate!

What is classmate?

What is Classmate, you may ask? Classmate is the first student discount app in Qatar, providing hefty discounts on numerous well-known brands in Qatar such as Papa John's, Krispy Kreme, and even Tim Hortons.

Why should I use Classmate?

The following are reasons why me and many students around Qatar use Classmate:

  • - Saves you money, especially if you're a student living on your own.
  • - New promotions and discounts are added from time to time.
  • - You're able to participate in amazing giveaways such as the iPad giveaway going on right now.
